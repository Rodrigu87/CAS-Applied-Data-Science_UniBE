# About Module 5 Ethics and best practices
provides a space for consolidating learning through peer discussions, critical reflection, and applied practice. Participants exchange insights on their Module 3 projects, engage with selected readings, and refine the direction of their final project. The module supports learners in integrating key concepts from the CAS, strengthening their analytical approach, and preparing for the successful completion of their applied data science project.

## Learning objectives
- Participants can assess and advise on a data science project
- Participants have an overview of Machine Learning techniques
- Participants understand what AI is and related aspects
- Participants have firts ideas of their final CAS project
