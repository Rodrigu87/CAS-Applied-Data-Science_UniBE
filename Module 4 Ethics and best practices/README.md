# About Module 4 Ethics and best practices
This module explores the ethical foundations and best practices of responsible data science. It covers issues such as privacy, fairness, transparency, and accountability, guiding participants to critically assess the societal impacts of data-driven systems..

## Learning objectives
- Understand key ethical principles related to data collection, analysis, and machine learning
- Identify and evaluate risks related to privacy, bias, fairness, and discrimination in data-driven systems.Identify and evaluate risks related to privacy, bias, fairness, and discrimination in data-driven systems.
- Apply best practices for transparency, reproducibility, and responsible data management.
- Integrate ethical considerations into the design, implementation, and communication of data science projects.
